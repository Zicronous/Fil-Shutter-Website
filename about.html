About Us

The contemporary FILIPINAS ASIA SHUTTER DOOR CORPORATION, often known as “FIL-SHUTTERS” (the parent company), founded in 1973, has its roots in Mr. Maximo Y. Chua, who aimed to produce a diverse range of metal products utilizing advanced techniques and methods. In the 1970s, we introduced in the construction industry the first roll-up shutter door.

    In 1983, at a time when metal doors were largely unheard of in the market, the company recognized the increasing demand for what we now call the "META FLUSH DOOR." That same year, it revolutionized the metal door industry by introducing Japanese manufacturing technology from the esteemed BUNKA SHUTTER CO. LTD., JAPAN. While using metal sheets for doors was considered impractical back then, the Metal Flush Door has since gained popularity and become a fundamental product in the industry across the nation.

Who We Are

We are FILIPINAS ASIA SHUTTER DOOR CORPORATION, a manufacturer that provides a wide range of high-quality MOTORIZED and MANUALLY OPERATED ROLL-UP DOORS, METAL DOORS, STEEL LOUVERS, GLASS DOORS and WINDOWS, BALUSTRADES, and much more.

Our company, FILIPINAS ASIA SHUTTER DOOR CORP. "FILSHUTTERS", has been a leader in the industry for 50+ years, positioning us as pioneers in this sector. We take pride in delivering exceptional products through innovative advancements and modern technology. Our dedicated and skilled workforce guarantees that we uphold the quality standards for which we are well-known.

Awards & Achievements

Recognition of Excellence & Industry Leadership

1983

Pioneering Recognition from Japan

The "PLAQUE OF PUTTING META FLUSH ON THE MARKET" citation was presented to FILIPINAS SHUTTERS by Mr. Hideo Sekimoto, President of BUNKA SHUTTER CO. LTD., JAPAN . Proof that our company started it all in the Philippine metal door industry.

1992

Exclusive European Partnership

BFT AUTOMATION OF ITALY granted the company the exclusive distributorship for GATE AUTOMATION SYSTEMS for sliding and swing gates, bringing European technology to the Philippines.

1998

International Safety Certification

Our "META FLUSH" door succeeded in obtaining the UNDERWRITERS LABORATORIES (UL) "PASSED" mark on FIRE RATING TEST a testament to our commitment to safety and quality.

2012

European Standard Achievement

Our Metal Door once again made history as the first in the Philippines to succeed in passing the EUROPEAN STANDARD of fire testing by TUV SUD of SINGAPORE.

2014

Industry Recognition

Filipinas Asia Shutter Door Corp. was recognized as the "PREFERRED BRAND FOR METAL DOORS AND WINDOWS" by BCI ASIA at the very first ARCHITECT'S CHOICE AWARD PHILIPPINES. This ceremony recognizes brand excellence in the nation's construction industry.
