About Us

The contemporary FILIPINAS ASIA SHUTTER DOOR CORPORATION, often known as “FIL-SHUTTER” (the parent company), founded in 1973, has its roots in Mr. Maximo Y. Chua, who aimed to produce a diverse range of metal products utilizing advanced techniques and methods. In the 1970s, we introduced in the construction industry the first roll-up shutter door.

    In 1983, at a time when metal doors were largely unheard of in the market, the company recognized the increasing demand for what we now call the "METAL FLUSH DOOR." That same year, it revolutionized the metal door industry by introducing Japanese manufacturing technology from the esteemed BUNKA SHUTTER CO. LTD., JAPAN. While using metal sheets for doors was considered impractical back then, the Metal Flush Door has since gained popularity and become a fundamental product in the industry across the nation.
