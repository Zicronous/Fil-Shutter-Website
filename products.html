Door Insulation Options

Enhance your doors' performance with our premium insulation options. Choose between honeycomb or rock wool for superior thermal and acoustic performance.

Honeycomb / Paper Core

The honeycomb or paper core insulation, is made from recycled paper; it is very lightweight and environmentally friendly. Ideal for interior doors. It effectively reduces sound within the room and provides structural strength, while also minimizing heat transfer.

  • Cost-Efficiency
  • Thermal insulation
  • Lightweight & durable
  • Sustainable
  • Eco-friendly
  • Basic sound dampening

Rock Wool / Mineral Wool

This insulation offers exceptional fire and weather resistance features. Primarily utilized in fire-rated metal doors for both industrial and commercial applications.

  • Outstanding thermal insulation

  • Fireproof (up to 1000°C)
  • Resistant to moisture
  • Non-flammable material
