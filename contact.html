Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our products and services

What is your typical lead time for door fabrication?

Standard products typically take 2-3 weeks for fabrication. Custom designs may require 4-6 weeks depending on complexity. Installation scheduling depends on site readiness.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation by our certified technicians. Installation is quoted separately based on project complexity and location.

What areas do you service?

We serve the entire Philippines, with major projects completed in Metro Manila, Laguna, Cavite, Batangas, Rizal, and Bulacan. We can coordinate nationwide delivery and installation.

What warranty do you offer on your products?

We offer a 1-year warranty on metal doors and RUD. Extended warranties are available for certain products and can be discussed with our sales team.
